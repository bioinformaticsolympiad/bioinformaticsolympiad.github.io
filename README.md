# Biology & Bioinformatics Olympiad 3.0 (2026) 🧬

**Official Website**: [bioinformaticsolympiad.github.io](https://bioinformaticsolympiad.github.io)

## About the Olympiad

The **Biology & Bioinformatics Olympiad 3.0** is a prestigious national-level competition organized by **BioPC** (A Bioinformatics Lab of Research & Training). This flagship event aims to inspire students in life sciences, promote scientific excellence, and provide a platform for exploring modern biology and bioinformatics through competitive events and hands-on learning.

Following the tremendous success of previous editions, BBO 3.0 brings together undergraduate and graduate students from across Bangladesh to test their knowledge, compete nationally, and explore career and research opportunities.

## 📋 Competition Structure

### **Stage 1: Online Elimination Round**
- **Format**: Preliminary screening conducted completely online and free of cost
- **Assessment**: Online quizzes and basic knowledge assessment
- **Platforms**: Google Meet, TestMoz, LiveMCQ
- **Eligibility**: Open to all undergraduate and graduate students of Life Sciences

### **Stage 2: Offline Final Round**
- **Format**: Advanced Biology and Bioinformatics challenges
- **Venue**: University of Chittagong (CU)
- **Selection**: Top scorers from the online round qualify automatically

## 🎓 Eligibility & Participation

### Who Can Participate?
- **Undergraduate students** from any recognized university
- **Graduate students** from any recognized university
- **Programs**: Biology, Biotechnology, Genetics, Microbiology, Bioinformatics, Pharmacy, Zoology, Botany, Marine Science, and related disciplines
- **Academic Level**: All years welcome (including freshers)
- **Requirement**: Individual participation only (no teamwork allowed)

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is there any registration fee?**  
A: The online round is completely free. If you qualify for the second round, a nominal fee applies for offline program support.

**Q: What is the registration deadline?**  
A: June 30, 2026 at 11:59 PM BST.

**Q: Which platforms will be used for the online round?**  
A: Google Meet, TestMoz, and LiveMCQ.

**Q: Where will the final round take place?**  
A: University of Chittagong (CU).

**Q: Can freshers or first-year students participate?**  
A: Absolutely! Students from all academic years are encouraged to join.

**Q: Is teamwork allowed?**  
A: No, the Olympiad is an individual competition.

**Q: How will participants be selected for the final round?**  
A: Participants with the highest scores in the online elimination round will qualify.

**Q: Will there be Bioinformatics questions for beginners?**  
A: Yes! The Olympiad includes both fundamental and advanced-level questions, accommodating students from different backgrounds.

For more FAQs, visit the [website](https://bioinformaticsolympiad.github.io#faq).
